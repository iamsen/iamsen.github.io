    Voyager

    Voyager helps you plan the optimal (fastest) route to travel to multiple locations. Examples: travellers visiting different sightseeing landmarks, explorers planning road trips, realtors scheduling open house visits, businesses optimizing their service routes. For all above cases, this Route Planner is a great choice for you!

    WannaPlan

    An app that helps you setup the next travel plan. Users can enter the places/restaurants they wanna visit, with date, time and other constraints. The app can help them plan out the best travel route to visit them.

    SDMS Project

    Statistical Decision Model for System Test -- a joint research project, between Research In Motion (RIM) and University of Waterloo, for improving the current Quality Assurance process of BlackBerry software products. The project is funded by MITACS and RIM.
